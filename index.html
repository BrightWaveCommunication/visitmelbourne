Practical Tips: Set Up Your Life in Melbourne
            • Get your Myki card for public transport:
              Buy a Myki card at train stations, 7-Eleven stores, or online. Register your card at ptv.vic.gov.au to check your balance and protect your funds.
              Prefer your phone? Set up Mobile Myki on Android via Google Wallet for easy tap-on/tap-off travel1.
